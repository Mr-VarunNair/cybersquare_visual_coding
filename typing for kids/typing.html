Why is Typing Important !

Typing is a fun and essential skill that helps kids express themselves, complete school assignments, and even explore the digital world faster and more efficiently. Learning typing at an early age prepares children for a technology driven future.

Practice Typing

What is Keyboard?

A keyboard is a tool we use to type letters, numbers, and symbols on a computer. It's like a magic board that helps you write stories, play games, and search for your favorite cartoons!

Basic Typing Tips


  • Start with the Home Row: Place your fingers on A, S, D, F, J, K, L, and ;.
  • Use All Fingers: Each finger controls specific keys. Avoid using only two fingers.
  • Keep Your Eyes on the Screen: Look at the screen while typing to develop muscle memory.
  • Practice Regularly: Spend a few minutes each day practicing typing games or lessons.
